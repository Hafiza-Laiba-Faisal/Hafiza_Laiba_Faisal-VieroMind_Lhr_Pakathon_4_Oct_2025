Privacy Policy
Your Privacy is Our Priority

NeuroShield is designed with privacy-first principles:

  • Anonymous IDs: We assign you a random anonymous ID. Your real identity is never stored.
  • Local Storage: All sensitive data (EEG, journals, chats) is stored locally on your device.
  • No Tracking: We don't use tracking cookies or analytics.
  • Opt-in Research: Research data sharing requires explicit consent and is always anonymous.
  • Data Security: Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard hashing.
  • Right to Delete: You can delete your account and all data at any time.
